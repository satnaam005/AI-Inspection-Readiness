# FDA Form 483 Theme Exploration

This document outlines the planned analysis workflow for exploring
documentation- and quality-system-related themes in FDA Form 483 observations.

## Planned Steps

1. Load publicly available Form 483 observation text.
2. Perform basic text cleaning.
3. Explore keyword frequency and TF-IDF-based themes.
4. Map observed themes to predefined inspection readiness risk signals.
5. Interpret findings through a quality and regulatory lens.
